Reading guide

The score (0–100) weights Identity 30 · Custody 30 · Material 25 · Risk 15; the tier is set on the lower bound of the 95% interval, so it is deliberately conservative. New evidence narrows the ± and can lift the tier — here, the provenance story, the receipt, and confirming the set is factory-sealed moved the report from Silver 85 ± 6 to Gold 93 ± 4. For sealed original product, the intact seal and the mint's own specification are treated as the material evidence — the method does not penalise you for not breaking a seal it would be wrong to break.
