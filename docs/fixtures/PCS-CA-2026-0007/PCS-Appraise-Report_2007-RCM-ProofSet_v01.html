Reading guide

The score (0–100) is the confidence that the object is what it's presented to be, weighted Identity 30 · Custody 30 · Material 25 · Risk 15. The ± is a 95% credible interval; the tier (Gold ≥80 / Silver ≥60 / Bronze ≥40) is assigned on the lower bound — so a wide interval on thin evidence reads honestly as Silver, not a false Gold. Missing checks widen the ±; they never lower the score. Coverage (views on file) is a separate axis from confidence. "Weight pending / held open" means we have not yet checked it — not that it failed. Every gap names the single action that closes it.
